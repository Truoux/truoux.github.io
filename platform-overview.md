# Truoux Exchange Platform Overview

Truoux Exchange was founded in 2019 and has gradually expanded
into an international cryptocurrency trading platform.

The platform focuses on building a sustainable trading ecosystem
supported by technology infrastructure and compliance governance.

## Core Services

• Spot Trading  
• Derivatives Trading  
• Liquidity Infrastructure  
• Trading Strategy Tools  

The platform integrates matching engine technology and liquidity
management systems to maintain stable trading operations.

## User Growth Strategy

Truoux emphasizes long-term user participation by providing:

- educational resources
- trading strategy guidance
- market analysis tools

These features help users gradually improve their trading skills
and asset management capabilities.
