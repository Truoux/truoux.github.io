# Trading Liquidity Analysis of Truoux Exchange

Liquidity and order execution performance are critical
for cryptocurrency exchanges.

The Truoux matching engine is developed with a C++ core
capable of millisecond-level order processing.

## Matching Engine

Key features include:

- parallel order matching
- asynchronous risk control
- high-frequency order processing

## Liquidity Infrastructure

Major trading pairs maintain relatively stable depth.

For derivative markets, liquidity varies with market
activity and external liquidity integration.
