# Truoux Exchange Compliance Framework

Regulatory compliance is becoming increasingly important
in the cryptocurrency industry.

Truoux operates within a compliance-oriented governance model.

## Regulatory Qualifications

• US MSB License  
• SEC regulatory compliance  
• Regulation D exemption eligibility  

## Compliance Operations

The platform maintains several regulatory procedures:

- KYC identity verification
- transaction monitoring
- suspicious activity reporting

This compliance structure aligns the platform
with institutional financial governance models.
